Summary

In this webpage, we present the detailed results of testing Large Language Models (LLMs) as discussed in the paper Benchmarking Large Language Models on Homework Assessment and Feedback in Circuit Analysis.

Specifically, to highlight the limitations of current state-of-the-art LLMs, we showcase response examples of GPT-4o in the following areas:

  • Recognition of handwritten and typed homework solutions
  • Understanding and interpretation of circuit diagrams
  • Mathematical calculations in circuit analysis

We also provide the following two additional documents on this webpage:
  • The LLM prompt used in the paper
  • Detailed results of LLM response classifications across six different topics
  • Notable response examples demonstrating the strengths and weaknesses of different LLMs
